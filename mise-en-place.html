About

A Legacy Built
Across Continents

Mise en Place is a premier Food & Beverage consultancy headquartered in Colombo, Sri Lanka. Our practice is led by a culinary director with over two decades of elite international experience spanning luxury resorts, five-star hotels, private royal households, and large-scale hospitality groups.

From directing culinary operations for Galle Face Group across 19 properties to serving as Culinary Director for H.H. The Emir of Qatar's private affairs during FIFA 2022, our founder brings a rare combination of operational mastery and strategic vision to every engagement.

We work with hotel groups, restaurant developers, F&B investors, and hospitality brands seeking to elevate their culinary identity, operational efficiency, and commercial performance.

"Great cuisine is not an accident. It is the result of precise preparation, disciplined systems, and unwavering standards — mise en place, in every sense."

Services

What We Deliver

We provide end-to-end F&B consultancy services — from pre-opening strategy to operational restructuring — for hotels, resorts, standalone restaurants, and institutional food service operators.

🍽 01
Culinary Strategy & Concept Development

Define your culinary identity. From concept to menu architecture, we build F&B propositions that resonate with market positioning and commercial objectives.

  • Brand and cuisine concept design
  • Menu engineering and development
  • Signature dish creation
  • Dietary and trend alignment
🏨 02
Hotel & Resort F&B Operations

Operational excellence for multi-outlet hotel F&B environments. We restructure teams, workflows, and systems to maximise consistency and profitability.

  • Kitchen operations review
  • Multi-outlet coordination
  • Pre-opening F&B setup
  • Food cost optimisation
📋 03
SOPs, Training & Quality Systems

Institutionalise quality. We design and implement SOPs, hygiene frameworks, staff training programmes, and quality audit systems built to international standards.

  • HACCP implementation
  • SOP and job aid development
  • Staff training programs
  • Food safety certification support
🎪 04
Events & VVIP Culinary Production

From intimate private dining to large-scale state events, we deliver flawless culinary experiences with precision production and full service management.

  • Private dining curation
  • Corporate event menus
  • Large-scale event catering
  • Protocol dining management
💰 05
F&B Investment & Business Advisory

For investors and developers entering the hospitality sector. We provide due diligence, feasibility assessment, and operational benchmarking to protect capital.

  • F&B feasibility studies
  • Investor-grade reporting
  • Supplier and procurement strategy
  • Revenue and cost benchmarking
🌿 06
New Product Development & Launch

From concept to commercial shelf. We lead new product development with structured feasibility testing, portion studies, shelf-life analysis, and market positioning.

  • Consumer and market research
  • Recipe and portion development
  • Photoshoot and launch support
  • Supplier development
