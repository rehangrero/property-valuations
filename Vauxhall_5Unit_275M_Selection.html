ADVISORY NOTES
🎯
Exit Sequence Strategy
Sell in this order: Apt 1 (A2 Floor 9) first — set your price benchmark with the most liquid unit. Then Apt 2 (A2 Floor 17) — use Apt 1's sale price as proof. Then Apt 3 (A1 Floor 13) — different buyer, no cannibalism. Then Apt 4 (B1 Floor 14). Lastly Apt 5 (B6 Floor 9) — the premium exit, priced highest, sold last.
👥
Four Buyer Profiles at Exit
A2 (ENE): Young professionals, first-home buyers, Colombo 02 lifestyle seekers. A1 (WSW): CBD workers, business professionals, corporate tenants. B1 (SSE): Couples and small families needing a second room, rental investors. B6 (NNW): Premium diaspora buyers, lifestyle investors, Beira Lake enthusiasts. You are not competing with yourself at any exit.
🧭
Why 4 Orientations Matters
Each orientation catches different light, different views, and different breeze. ENE and NNW units are cooler — lower electricity bills, more comfortable living. SSE and WSW units get more sun — warmer but with more city exposure. Offering buyers a choice of orientation at exit is a differentiator when competing against single-orientation sellers.
💡
A-Type Units: Often Overlooked
The A1 and A2 units are frequently passed over by investors chasing larger sizes. This is a mistake. At LKR 53–54M negotiated, they offer the best capital efficiency in the building — and the largest buyer pool at exit. 500 sqft is the right size for the Colombo 02 rental market, where single professionals and couples dominate demand.
⚖️
This vs the LKR 350M Portfolio
The LKR 350M selection (B7 + B3) has a higher absolute return: LKR 293M base case vs LKR 219M here. But this portfolio deploys LKR 54M less capital. The return on incremental capital deployed in the 350M portfolio is approximately 37% over 5 years — positive, but lower than this portfolio's return on its own deployed capital.
📋
Before You Sign
Floor allocations must be in the written offer letter — not verbal. Confirm exact unit positions on the floor plate for A1 and A2 — they sit on opposite sides of the core. Independent title search before SPA. Independent attorney review of all five SPAs. Do not pay reservation without written confirmation of floor, unit, and price per unit.
